About Us

GANESH SALES AND SUPPLY COMPANY, the trusted name for Engineering and Industrial Tools, established in 1943, is one of the leading Trading Companies for various precision measuring instruments & technical tools.

We are the master distributors for brands like INSIZE, TOOLFAST, TORQUE MASTER, APEX, TRUTHREAD, KTA, TURNMAX, BAGSON, CP-GRAT-EX, and many more. Having customers in India and overseas, GANESH SALES AND SUPPLY COMPANY is enjoying excellent relations with all its customers and will do our best to maintain these relationships on a long-term basis.

Recently, we have stepped into the field of e-commerce in order to provide a wide range of industrial tools at wholesale prices all across the country, including the most remote areas.

We have tried our best to present all relevant information about our products on our website, but if you still need any other information, you may contact us at any time.

In the changing scenario, we recognized the need for distributing quality Tools and Equipment at a low cost supported by excellent after-sales service to dealers across the country. With a great deal of customization, tailored packages, and a professional approach, we assure you that the rates offered by us are the most competitive ex-factory price in the Industry, supplemented by timely service.

As we enter the 60th year of service, we take this opportunity to salute all our customers and thank them for their association with us and the support provided throughout this journey.

We look forward to your continuous support in the years to come.

Thanking you and assuring our best services at all times.
